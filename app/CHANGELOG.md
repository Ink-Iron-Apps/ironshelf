# Changelog

All notable user-visible changes to the Ironshelf app are documented here, newest first.

## 0.1.0

### Added
- Sign in with your Ironshelf cloud account and pick which of your servers to open — reach your library from anywhere, no URLs to type.
- Read your books right in the app: built-in EPUB, PDF, and comic (CBZ) readers.
- Reading progress syncs with your server and across devices — pick up on your phone where you left off on the web.
- EPUB reader with light, dark, and sepia themes, adjustable text size, and a table of contents.
- Comic reader with pinch-to-zoom and tap-to-turn pages.
- Reading status everywhere: progress bars on in-progress books and a badge on the ones you've finished.
- Mark books as read or unread from the book page.
- Continue Reading shelf on the home screen opens straight into the reader.
